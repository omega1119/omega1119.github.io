About Us

Greenwood IT Consultancy is a London-based software consultancy with over two decades of hands-on engineering experience. We specialise in complex, long-lived systems where reliability, security, and delivery under real-world constraints matter more than novelty.

Our expertise spans the full stack, from Azure cloud architecture and DevOps pipelines to native iOS/macOS development with Swift and SwiftUI, and down to real-time audio DSP with C++ and JUCE. We've delivered solutions for government departments, enterprise clients, and the creative industries alike.

Whether it's stabilising critical infrastructure, building AI-powered document systems with RAG and vector search, or crafting precision music tools, we bring disciplined design, careful state management, and a bias toward maintainable, testable code.
