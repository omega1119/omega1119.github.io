BeatBar Pocket: Portable Timing Tools for Musicians

BeatBar Pocket brings the essentials to iPhone, making tempo and timing calculations effortless.

Features

  • Tap tempo and BPM↔ms note value conversion
  • Note↔frequency calculator (A4-tuning aware)
  • Transpose and speed helpers
  • Quick access widgets
  • Clean, mobile-first design
  • Backed by the same Swift packages as the macOS app

Whether you're practicing, teaching, or performing, BeatBar Pocket is your go-to timing toolkit.
