BeatLab for iOS: Mobile Theory Tools

BeatLab for iOS focuses on making music theory accessible and interactive.

Features

  • Ranked scale and chord finders
  • Notes-in-key and scale chord tools
  • Key/chord overlap and mode pivot analysis
  • Touch-friendly, intuitive interface
  • Powered by BeatCore and reusable SwiftUI components

Ideal for students, teachers, and musicians who want to explore theory on the go.
